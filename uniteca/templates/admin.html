    Hi admin! on this page you can add/remove books, check reservations and loan books. Use the navbar at the top-left to redirect you to various sections of the page.

    In particular:


    1. Home: It redirect you in this page;
    2. Book: It redirect you in the book's page, where you can add/remove any books;
    3. Reservetion: It redirects you to the reservations page, where you can check all the reservations made and available places;
    4. Check: It redirects you to the loaned books page, where you can check all the loaned books;
    5. Logout: It redirects you to the homepage of this site.
